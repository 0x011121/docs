If you receive the error **"You HWID HAS CHANGED"**, it means your current **Hardware ID (HWID)** does not match the unique ID recorded when you first successfully logged into the loader.

## HWID Troubleshooting

Your HWID is a unique key derived from your PC's components (CPU, Motherboard, storage devices, etc.). The loader is tied to this key.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request HWID Reset">
    Submit an HWID reset request if you have changed hardware or can no longer access the software on your current system.
  </Step>
</Steps>
