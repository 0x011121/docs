The Advanced Magic Bullet system is built for precision and deep customization, allowing experienced users to fine-tune behavior for smooth, natural, and highly optimized performance.

## How it Works

The system uses advanced prediction and targeting logic to deliver smooth, accurate, and responsive performance while remaining fully customizable to your preferred playstyle.

<Note>
  **What is a Hit Box?**

  A hit box is the invisible area around a player model where shots are registered by the game. The Magic Bullet system works by improving shot registration logic and target prediction, effectively making hits more consistent and responsive during gameplay.
</Note>

### ⚠️ Important Requirement

<Warning>
  **USING MAGIC BULLET**

  The feature is designed for stable and legitimate-looking gameplay; however, PUBG server-side protections are stricter than before, and excessively high ranges may increase the risk of temporary 24 Hrs. restrictions. For a more legitimate experience, most users prefer values between 1.7 and 2.0.
</Warning>
